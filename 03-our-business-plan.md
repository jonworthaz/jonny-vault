# 03 — Our Business Plan

*Concrete decisions: what we sell, to whom, at what price, and how we win.*

## The product shape we're building

A **vertical AI-powered micro-SaaS / digital subscription**, sold online, billed
monthly, distributed primarily through affiliates. This is the cleanest mapping
of the Medvi engine onto a non-regulated, geo-agnostic, ~95%-margin substrate.

Why this shape:
- **Recurring by nature** (law #1).
- **~100% margin** → we can out-pay every affiliate (law #2).
- **AI-buildable by a 2-person team** (law #3) — the product *is* AI, so the same
  tools that run the company also are the company.
- **Instant global delivery** — geo-agnostic with zero logistics.
- **Demand tailwind** — the AI-tools category is the Ozempic-scale wave of now.

## Choosing the exact niche (the build-then-decide gate)

We do **not** marry a niche on day one. We run a 2-week demand-validation sprint
and let data choose. Candidate niches (all recurring, all affiliate-friendly, all
buildable with current AI APIs) — the full, scored set lives on the
[Idea Board (09)](./09-idea-board.md):

| Candidate | The recurring job it does | Why it retains |
|---|---|---|
| **A. Creator content engine** | Turns one input (a topic/URL) into a month of platform-native posts, scripts, thumbnails | Creators post forever; daily need |
| **B. Niche SEO/content writer** | Bulk, on-brand long-form + programmatic SEO for SMBs/agencies | Content is never "done" |
| **C. Sales/outbound copilot** | Researches prospects + drafts personalised outreach at scale | Pipeline is a perpetual need |
| **D. Vertical "GPT" for a profession** | e.g. tradespeople quotes, tutors' lesson plans, realtor listings | Daily workflow tool |

**Selection criteria (scored in the sprint):** search/social demand trend, CPC
(proxy for buyer intent + monetisation), competitor pricing headroom, how
defensibly good we can make the AI output, and affiliate fit.

### Recommended lead
**A or C.** Both have daily-use retention, obvious affiliate pools (creators
promote creator tools; sales influencers promote sales tools), and clear "ride
the wave" demand. The sprint confirms which.

## Positioning

- **One painful job, done 10x cheaper/faster than a human or a generic chatbot.**
- Not "an AI tool" — "the thing that does *[specific outcome]* for *[specific
  person]*." Specificity converts and retains.
- Honest, concrete before/after (real outputs, real users — see Guardrails).

## Pricing (the transparent Medvi hook)

| Tier | Price | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Trial / intro | **$1 for 7 days** or **50% off month 1** | Clear, dated, disclosed step-up |
| Core | **$39/month** | The default |
| Pro | **$99/month** | Higher limits / seats |
| Annual | **2 months free** | Pulls cash forward, cuts churn |

Rules we lock from the Medvi pricing lesson:
- **Monthly = 30 days, not 28.** No padded cadence.
- The post-trial price is shown *before* payment, in plain language.
- One-click cancel. Cancellation is the cheapest churn-reduction insurance we have.

## Who runs it

- **You + (optionally) one other person**, mirroring Matthew + Elliot.
- Everything else is AI agents + a small contractor bench for spot work
  (a part-time compliance/legal advisor, a designer on call).

## What "winning" looks like (north-star)

A subscription business where:
1. Affiliate-driven new MRR > affiliate cost within ~2 months (payback).
2. 6-month logo retention ≥ 50% (the Medvi threshold, applied to us).
3. Founder time goes to *acquisition + product*, not ops.
