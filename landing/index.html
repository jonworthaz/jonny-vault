MarkUp Tools

Two browser-based tools for marking up photos — nothing to install, everything stays on your device. Pick the one that fits the job.

Tool 1 · Quick

MarkUp

Single-image annotation
Best for: marking up one photo, fast.
  • Upload one image and annotate it
  • Circle, box, highlight, arrow, freehand, text boxes, numbered markers
  • Numbered items list with part name, note & cost (single running total)
  • Crop, zoom/pinch, pan, undo/redo
  • Save / Save as / Copy image, plus CSV and HTML report
Open MarkUp →
Tool 2 · Full report

Damage Assessment

Multi-photo vehicle damage builder
Best for: a complete vehicle damage report.
  • Many photos in one job — each named (Car front, Bonnet, Tyres…)
  • Vehicle details: registration, make, model, year, colour
  • Optional auto-numbering on every tool, not just the number tool
  • Currency MYR (RM) or USD
  • One full report across all photos — PDF & spreadsheet, with per-photo subtotals and a grand total
  • All the same drawing, crop, zoom & markup tools
Open Damage Assessment →
Tool 3 · Accessible

Damage Assessment · Large

High-visibility version
Best for: low-res laptops & easier-to-see text.
  • Exactly the same as Damage Assessment
  • Bigger text and buttons throughout
  • Higher contrast and clear keyboard focus outlines
  • Tuned to fit smaller / lower-resolution screens
