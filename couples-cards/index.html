A phone battery dies. A paper photo creases and yellows. Steel doesn't.

We think the moments worth keeping deserve a material worth keeping them in. Not a screen you scroll past — something with weight, that lives against you, that you'll still be carrying in thirty years. And because the best moments belong to two people, we only ever make them in pairs.

07 — good to know

The questions couples ask.

Can you colourise an old black-and-white photo?+

Yes — that's some of our favourite work. Send the best scan or photo of the print you have; our studio restores damage, balances the tones and colourises it, then shows you the result on steel before we make anything. If it can't be done well, we'll tell you honestly rather than print something we're not proud of.

Why do you only sell them in pairs?+

Because the whole idea is two people carrying the same moment apart. A pair is the product. If you genuinely need a single, just ask — but the boxes, the pricing and the point are all built around two.

Will it really survive in a wallet?+

It's brushed stainless steel at 0.8 mm — about the thickness of two bank cards. It won't crease, tear or yellow the way paper does, and the brushed finish hides everyday marks. It's a keepsake built to be carried, not a display piece to keep in a drawer.

How long does a pair take?+

Each pair is made to order, so allow time for the studio retouch, your preview approval, and printing. You'll get a clear date at checkout — and for gifts, you can schedule delivery for the day that matters.
