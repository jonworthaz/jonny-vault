Six plimsoll styles built on one shared last and one shared outsole platform — a deliberate cost decision that lets the range launch on a single set of tooling. Every style is engineered to be machine washable: cement-free bonds, closed-cell foam, no leather, colourfast trims. The brand signature is a coloured pinstripe along the sole edge, recoloured per colourway.

Read me first
All measurements are for size UK 7 and are development targets, not final graded specs. Drawings are technical flats (not to scale unless dimensioned). All wash, bond, flex and slip figures are targets to be confirmed by physical testing per §5 before any "machine washable" claim is made.

Construction sequence
  1. Cut — die-cut canvas to grain; cut mesh, gore, reinforcements.
  2. Close upper — join quarters to vamp, insert gore, bind collar, stitch toe cap (8–9 SPI).
  3. Strobel — stitch strobel sock to lasting margin; set TPU counter + toe puff.
  4. Last & heat-set — mount PL-01, steam, heat-set profile.
  5. Prep bond — rough + water-based primer on margin & outsole.
  6. Attach sole — position cup outsole, apply foxing + pinstripe bead, press.
  7. Vulcanise — cure foxing bond; chill-set.
  8. De-last & finish — insert footbed, clean, QC + lot wash-test, box.
