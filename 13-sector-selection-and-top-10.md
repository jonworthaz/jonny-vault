# 13 — Where to Point the Channel: Sectors + the Top 10 Content Concepts

*The decision. This turns the [70/30 playbook](./12-70-30-ai-channel-playbook.md)'s
method into an answer: the **best sectors to target** and the **ten most
profitable, currently-rising content concepts** (the actual subject matter) to
build the channel around. Synthesised from three fresh expert research passes —
sub-niche economics, whitespace/competition, and rising subject matter — all
web-sourced 19 Jul 2026; figures are directional benchmarks, flagged at the end.*

## The one insight that decides everything

> **The uncrowded high-value niches are empty for the *same* reason — a high
> accuracy, regulatory, or language bar that lazy AI can't clear. Post the 2026
> "inauthentic content" crackdown, that bar isn't the obstacle to your moat; it
> *is* the moat.** Pick the sector whose bar *you* (plus the human 30%) can
> clear, and the thin competition is handed to you.

Two more rules the data made non-negotiable:
- **Ad RPM is the appetiser; the affiliate/lead overlay is the meal.** Finance
  tops out ~$15–35 RPM, but one insurance customer is worth >$10k LTV, a legal
  lead averages ~$132 (highest of any industry), an advisor client CAC is
  ~$3,119. **Target sectors where you can sell a lead or a recurring signup, not
  just rent eyeballs to AdSense.**
- **Lock every channel to ONE persona/vertical.** The "wide-open sub-niche"
  signal in the data is always specific ("debt payoff for single parents,"
  "AI agents for realtors"), never generic.

Context: faceless is now **~38% of new creator monetisation ventures** (up from
12% in 2022) at **<$3/video** — cheap to enter, so the edge is niche + accuracy,
not production.

**The geography multiplier (applies to any sector above):** most operators
default to US-generic. Deliberately targeting **Australia (CPM ~$36, the
highest on Earth) and Canada ($29)** captures premium rates at materially lower
competition, **zero translation risk** — the single highest effort-adjusted
arbitrage in the research. The **German/DACH** version pays 2–3× US in
finance/tech but needs a native-language review pass (the barrier moved from
voice, now studio-quality, to *editorial idiom* — which is exactly why it stays
open).

**Best risk-adjusted sector = #1/#2 combined: a retirement-finance-and-insurance
channel** (they're sister audiences), optionally in the AU/CA arbitrage. It
stacks the platform's highest ad rates + the highest-LTV lead-gen + a genuine,
content-resetting 2026 tailwind, with near-zero faceless competition.

## Part B — The Top 10 content concepts (the subject matter)

Ranked by **profit × rising-momentum × low-competition**. Each is a specific,
screenshot-safe, faceless-buildable channel — not a category.

### ★ 1. The Retirement Tax-Window — pre-RMD strategy for the Peak 65 wave
- **What it's about:** animated case-study walkthroughs of the retirement-
  transition tax playbook — Roth-conversion "bracket filling," the gap-years
  window before RMDs (73/75), IRMAA/Medicare premium traps, Social Security
  timing. ("How the Johnsons converted $133k tax-free.")
- **Why it's rising:** **4.1M Americans turn 65 in 2026 (~11,000/day)**; the
  2024–27 "Peak 65" surge is the largest ever, and **OBBBA (Jul 2025) reset the
  conversion maths** — so 2026 content is genuinely new and being re-searched.
- **The money:** highest-RPM corner of finance + **financial-advisor and
  annuity/LTC lead-gen** (retainers cited at $3–7k/mo) + retirement-planning
  software. **Competition: LOW** (talking-head advisors only). Faceless-fit:
  perfect — it's maths + charts, and the 55+ audience has the highest ad LTV.

### ★ 2. AI Agents for [one vertical] — build-alongs, realtors first
- **What it's about:** concrete, screen-recorded automation builds for ONE
  vertical at a time ("Build a listing-follow-up AI agent in n8n for realtors,"
  "auto-draft CMA reports"), then sister channels for lawyers/med-spas.
- **Why it's rising:** **72% of real-estate pros now use ≥1 AI tool** (was <30%
  two years ago), **85% plan to raise AI spend in 2026**; the market is pivoting
  generative → agentic. n8n/agent tutorial demand is exploding.
- **The money:** two stacked layers — **SaaS recurring affiliate** (n8n/Make/CRM,
  up to 40–60% lifetime) + **AI-automation-agency retainers** ($3–7k/mo, low
  competition). **Competition: MED** generic, **LOW** vertical-locked. Faceless
  by nature — you're demoing AI building AI.

### ★ 3. Credit-Card Points Optimizer — redemption maths, not lifestyle
- **What it's about:** "best transfer partner for this route," "is Bilt's
  dual-currency worth it?", "Chase Points Boost real cent-per-point maths" —
  data-viz comparisons, no travel vlogging.
- **Why it's rising:** 2026 is a churn year — **Bilt launched 3 new cards
  (Jan 2026)**, avg bonuses up ~6% YoY, new issuer travel portals. Complexity =
  search demand.
- **The money:** **$25–200+ per approved application** (premium cards to $405);
  "best card for X" converts highest. **Competition: HIGH** overall, **LOW–MED**
  for faceless AI refresh content. Evergreen + re-shootable each product change.

### ★ 4. Money Dysmorphia — psychology-of-money for high-earning millennials/Gen Z
- **What it's about:** documentary-style explainers on *feeling broke while doing
  fine* — BNPL traps, lifestyle creep, "disillusionomics." Behavioural, brand-
  safe, not stock picks.
- **Why it's rising:** a genuinely trending 2026 term — **43% of Gen Z / 41% of
  millennials** report money dysmorphia; heavy Jan–Jun 2026 press. Mostly covered
  in articles, not video — **Competition: LOW.**
- **The money:** personal-finance CPM + budgeting/HYSA/net-worth apps (Chime 10%
  of MRR for 18 months; Credit Karma bounties). Proven high-retention narrated
  format with a viral hook.

### ★ 5. Perimenopause / Hormone Health, Evidence-Led — for women 40+
- **What it's about:** citation-first explainers — HRT myths vs 2026 guidelines,
  which supplements actually have RCT evidence, symptom tracking. "What the
  studies say," no medical claims.
- **Why it's rising:** one of the fastest-growing categories in health/wellness —
  perimenopause market **$4.77B → $7.22B**; the space is shifting **influencer-led
  → evidence-led**, the exact gap a rigorous faceless channel fills.
- **The money:** supplements = ~49% of the market → high-margin affiliate +
  telehealth-HRT lead-gen + femtech. **Competition: LOW–MED.** Faceless *removes*
  the "are they a doctor?" trust problem by being clinical, not personal.
  *Discipline flag: cite everything or risk demonetisation.*

### 6. SMB Cybersecurity in Plain English — threat explainers for business owners
- **What it's about:** "the scam hitting small businesses this month + the 3
  settings that stop it," AI-phishing explainers, compliance in plain English —
  for *owners*, not IT pros.
- **Why it's rising:** SMB security spend **→$109B**; **43% of attacks hit small
  business, 41% AI-driven, 63% raised budgets.** Fear + budget = demand.
  **Competition: LOW** for the owner-facing angle.
- **The money:** premium B2B CPM + security-SaaS recurring affiliate + MSP/cyber-
  insurance leads. Screen-safe, fear-driven, evergreen.

### 7. Insurance & Planning for the Newly-Retired — Peak 65 sister to #1
- **What it's about:** Medicare Advantage vs Original + IRMAA, annuities decoded,
  LTC planning, "the 6 decisions in your first year of retirement." Explainer +
  checklist.
- **Why it's rising:** same Peak 65 tailwind — 11,000 retirement decisions/day
  through 2027. **The money:** **insurance is the single highest-RPM finance
  corner (CPM $27–60)** + large per-lead Medicare/annuity/LTC payouts.
  **Competition: LOW** — unsexy, so under-served despite huge ad budgets.

### 8. AI-Proof Your Career — per-role playbooks + the high-income skill of the week
- **What it's about:** concrete "make YOUR [accountant/paralegal/marketer] role
  AI-resistant," salary/interview breakdowns, WEF-flagged rising skills, job-
  growth data-viz. Not doom-scrolling.
- **Why it's rising:** heavy 2026 press cycle (WEF: **170M new vs 92M displaced
  jobs by 2030; cyber roles +32%**). **Competition: MED** generic, **LOW** as
  per-role playbooks with real BLS/WEF numbers.
- **The money:** career/upskilling CPM + course/cert affiliates (Coursera),
  résumé-AI tools, job-board CPA. Cheap to produce at volume across roles.

### 9. Dividend & Passive-Income Portfolio, Data-First
- **What it's about:** "this $X portfolio paid $Y this month" data-viz series,
  dividend-growth-vs-yield backtests, REIT/covered-call ETF breakdowns, FIRE
  maths. Charts only.
- **Why it's rising:** persistent, proven faceless finance format with steady
  2026 demand. **The money:** finance CPM + brokerage affiliates (Webull $50–75,
  M1, Robinhood) + portfolio-tracker SaaS + HYSA ($250–300/lead). **Competition:
  HIGH** — ranked lower for saturation; win only via a sub-angle ("dividends for
  UK-US expats," "monthly-payer REIT tracker").

### 10. GLP-1 & Metabolic Health, Science-Explainer *(brand-safe)*
- **What it's about:** "what the 2026 trials actually found," GLP-1 beyond weight
  loss (aging, sleep apnea, liver), muscle-preservation. Journalistic, not
  prescribing.
- **Why it's rising:** huge 2026 science flow (a Nature Comms RCT: semaglutide
  slowed an epigenetic aging clock ~9%; Harvard coverage); **73% rate healthy
  aging important.** **Competition: MED** (most is influencer testimonial; a
  cited explainer is differentiated).
- **The money:** health CPM + telehealth GLP-1 lead-gen + protein/electrolyte
  affiliate + metabolic apps. *Strongest discipline flag: educational + cited
  only, or it's a demonetisation/brand-safety risk (see [07 — Guardrails](./07-guardrails.md)).*

**Runners-up (real, a notch lower):** 11) sleep-optimisation supplement reviews ·
12) bookkeeping/financial-ops for a niche business owner (QuickBooks/Xero
recurring) · 13) debt-payoff for a specific persona (named "wide-open" in the
data) · 14) AI tools for lawyers/med-spas (a #2 sister, not a rival).

## The recommendation

**Two clear winners — pick by which bar you can personally clear:**

1. **#1 Retirement Tax-Window (+ #7 as its sister feed).** The best overall:
   platform-highest ad rates, highest-value lead-gen, a 2024–27 tailwind that
   *resets old content*, near-zero faceless competition. Bar to clear: financial-
   modelling accuracy (the human 30%). Run it in the **AU/CA arbitrage** for an
   extra CPM multiplier.
2. **#2 AI Agents for Realtors.** The best if you're technical and want *recurring*
   revenue over ad revenue — 40–60% lifetime SaaS commissions + agency retainers
   compound hardest, and you're demoing on-screen by nature.

If you want maximum brand-safety with less modelling depth: **#4 Money Dysmorphia**
or **#6 SMB Cybersecurity** — both low-competition, high-pay, screenshot-safe.

**How it plugs into the vault:** every concept above is disclosed, SFW, and
accuracy-anchored — it clears **Gate 2** and **Guardrail #1** cleanly (unlike the
NSFW persona in [11](./11-ai-influencer-play.md)), and it's monetised by the
overlay ladder in [12](./12-70-30-ai-channel-playbook.md) (recurring affiliate +
lead-gen). **Next action:** run the top pick through doc 12's 5-gate "is it
PAYING?" test and the weekly radar for two cycles to confirm the format before
committing production — then hit the [scale gate](./06-economics-and-funnel.md).
