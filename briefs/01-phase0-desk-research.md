# Brief 01 — Phase-0 demand-signal desk research
*Status: active — next up · owner: CEO session*

- **Objective:** score the three shortlisted niches from
  [09-idea-board.md](../09-idea-board.md) — #01 Creator content engine,
  #02 Sales copilot, #04 Vertical GPT — on real external data, and recommend
  the order for fake-door testing.
- **Serves the priority how?** It is the first step of the current priority
  (Phase 0, [08-roadmap.md](../08-roadmap.md)).
- **Definition of done:**
  - A comparison table covering, per niche: search/social demand trajectory,
    CPC range (buyer-intent proxy), 3+ competitors with pricing, and named
    affiliate-pool evidence.
  - Every number/claim carries a source link and a confidence level.
  - A recommended test order with a one-paragraph rationale.
- **Verification:** `review-qa` spot-checks 3 randomly chosen claims against
  their sources; all 3 must hold.
- **Budget:** one session; `research` department; output is a doc
  (`briefs/reports/01-desk-research.md`). No tooling built.
- **Kill criteria:** none — this brief always produces a decision input.
