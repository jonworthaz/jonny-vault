# Brief 03 — Traffic test & Phase-0 gate readout
*Status: queued (opens when Brief 02 closes) · owner: CEO session + founder*

- **Objective:** put a small, capped signal source behind each fake-door page —
  capped paid traffic and/or manual outreach to 20 target buyers per niche
  ([08-roadmap.md](../08-roadmap.md)) — and produce the Phase-0 gate readout.
- **Serves the priority how?** It IS the Phase-0 gate.
- **Definition of done:**
  - A one-page readout per niche: visits, signups, landing→signup %, cost per
    signup (if paid), plus any reply/objection notes from outreach.
  - A recorded Go / Hold / Recycle / Kill decision per niche, with rationale,
    logged in [Claude Ideas](../claude-ideas/) or the readout itself.
- **Verification:** every number in the readout traces to an analytics export
  or an outreach log — none from memory. `review-qa` reconciles the readout
  against the raw exports.
- **Budget:** spend cap set by the founder at the board review before any
  spend starts. Founder runs/approves outreach sends and any ad account spend.
- **Gate (from 08):** >10–15% landing→signup on one niche → open Phase 1
  build briefs. All niches below threshold → recycle to the idea board and
  promote the next candidate.
