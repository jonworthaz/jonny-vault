# 08 — Roadmap

*A 90-day build-then-decide plan. Minimal spend until the data earns the spend.*

## Phase 0 — Niche selection sprint (Week 1–2)

**Goal:** pick the exact product from the candidates in [03](./03-our-business-plan.md).

- [ ] Score candidate niches on demand trend, buyer intent (CPC), competitor
      pricing headroom, AI output quality we can hit, affiliate fit.
- [ ] Validate demand cheaply: 3–5 fake-door landing pages + small traffic test,
      or manual outreach to 20 target buyers. Measure email/waitlist conversion.
- [ ] **Gate:** one niche shows clear pull (e.g. >10–15% landing→signup) → proceed.

## Phase 1 — Build the machine (Week 3–5)

**Goal:** a working product + funnel + billing, on minimal spend.

- [ ] Core product MVP (the one job, done excellently) — AI-built.
- [ ] Funnel: landing → live mini-demo → checkout (Stripe trial/intro).
- [ ] Onboarding that delivers a "win" within minutes.
- [ ] Lifecycle emails (welcome, value, pre-renewal reminder).
- [ ] AI support + human escalation inbox.
- [ ] Analytics (PostHog) + Stripe metrics wired.
- [ ] Terms, privacy, refund policy, cookie consent live ([07](./07-guardrails.md)).

## Phase 2 — Validate retention with affiliates (Week 6–9)

**Goal:** prove the unit economics on a small, honest cohort.

- [ ] Stand up affiliate program (Rewardful/FirstPromoter/Tolt).
- [ ] Recruit 5–20 hand-picked niche affiliates; supply approved creative.
- [ ] Build the AI creative machine (copy + image + video variants).
- [ ] Run the cohort. Track trial→paid, CAC, M1/M3 retention.
- [ ] Weekly cohort readout; iterate product on churn-reason data.
- [ ] **Gate (the scale gate, from [06](./06-economics-and-funnel.md)):**
      payback ≤ 3mo · M1 retention ≥ 70% · M3 trending ≥ 55% · ≥1 repeatable source.

## Phase 3 — Decide & scale (Week 10–12+)

**If the gate is met → Medvi mode:**
- [ ] Open the affiliate program; list on CPA networks.
- [ ] Turn on paid social, throttled strictly to proven payback.
- [ ] Flood honest creative across channels (the "5,000 ads" capability).
- [ ] Add annual plan + referral loop to lift LTV.
- [ ] Start the SEO/content flywheel for compounding free traffic.

**If the gate is missed → fix or pivot:**
- [ ] Retention < target → improve product/onboarding/habit before any spend.
- [ ] CAC/payback off → re-test offer, price, or creative.
- [ ] No pull at all → return to Phase 0, pick the next niche. Cheap to do early.

## Operating rhythm (ongoing)

| Cadence | What |
|---|---|
| Daily | Watch trials, support escalations, creative performance |
| Weekly | AI-generated metrics readout; kill losing creative, scale winners; pay/recruit affiliates |
| Monthly | Cohort retention review; pricing/packaging tests; compliance spot-check |
| Quarterly | Channel mix review; new feature/vertical bets; advisor review of claims |

## The two questions that govern every decision

1. **Does it compound?** (recurring revenue, owned audience, SEO, affiliate
   relationships) — prioritise. One-off hacks — deprioritise.
2. **Would it survive a screenshot?** ([07](./07-guardrails.md)) — if not, don't
   ship it. That's the line between a durable engine and a Medvi-style flameout.

---

### Summary of the whole vault

> Copy Medvi's **operating system** — 2 people + AI, outsource the heavy layer,
> own acquisition + billing, win distribution through affiliates paid from a fat
> margin. Apply it to a **digital subscription** (higher margin, geo-agnostic,
> lower risk). Build the machine first, prove **retention** before spending, and
> hold the **guardrails** that keep the business alive where Medvi's is unravelling.
